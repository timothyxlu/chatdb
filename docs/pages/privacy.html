Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

ChatDB Extension is designed to help users save AI chat conversations into their own ChatDB service. We keep data collection minimal and transparent.

Information We Process

  • Conversation content from supported AI chat pages when you explicitly trigger capture.
  • Basic metadata such as source URL, title, and timestamps for saved sessions.
  • Your ChatDB API token, stored locally in the extension to authenticate uploads.

How We Use Information

  • To upload selected conversation data to your self-hosted ChatDB instance.
  • To provide extension functionality, including manual capture and sync actions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data and we do not share conversation data with third-party advertisers. Data is sent only to the ChatDB server endpoint configured by you.

Data Retention

Data retention is controlled by your ChatDB deployment. You can delete sessions or revoke tokens from your ChatDB settings at any time.

Security

We recommend using HTTPS for your ChatDB endpoint and rotating API tokens periodically. Keep your browser and extension up to date.
