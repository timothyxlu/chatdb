隐私政策 Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026 年 3 月 3 日 Last updated: March 3, 2026

ChatDB Extension 旨在帮助用户将 AI 对话保存到自己的 ChatDB 服务。我们坚持最小化、透明化的数据处理原则。 ChatDB Extension is designed to help users save AI chat conversations into their own ChatDB service. We keep data collection minimal and transparent.

我们处理的信息 Information We Process

  • 当你主动触发保存时，处理来自支持页面的对话内容。 Conversation content from supported AI chat pages when you explicitly trigger capture.
  • 会话来源链接、标题和时间戳等基础元数据。 Basic metadata such as source URL, title, and timestamps for saved sessions.
  • 用于上传认证的 ChatDB API Token（仅存储在扩展本地）。 Your ChatDB API token, stored locally in the extension to authenticate uploads.

信息用途 How We Use Information

  • 将你选择的对话数据上传到你的自托管 ChatDB 实例。 To upload selected conversation data to your self-hosted ChatDB instance.
  • 提供扩展核心功能，包括手动保存与同步操作。 To provide extension functionality, including manual capture and sync actions.

数据共享 Data Sharing

我们不会出售个人数据，也不会向广告商共享对话内容。数据仅发送到你配置的 ChatDB 服务端地址。 We do not sell personal data and we do not share conversation data with third-party advertisers. Data is sent only to the ChatDB server endpoint configured by you.

数据保留 Data Retention

数据保留策略由你的 ChatDB 部署决定。你可以随时在 ChatDB 设置中删除会话或吊销 Token。 Data retention is controlled by your ChatDB deployment. You can delete sessions or revoke tokens from your ChatDB settings at any time.

安全建议 Security

建议为 ChatDB 端点启用 HTTPS，并定期轮换 API Token。同时保持浏览器和扩展为最新版本。 We recommend using HTTPS for your ChatDB endpoint and rotating API tokens periodically. Keep your browser and extension up to date.
